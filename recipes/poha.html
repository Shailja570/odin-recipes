POHA

DESCRIPTION

Poha is a light, fluffy, and flavorful Indian dish made from flattened rice. It's especially popular in states like Maharashtra, Madhya Pradesh, and Gujarat, and is often eaten for breakfast or as a quick snack.

The flattened rice is rinsed and softened, then cooked with mustard seeds, curry leaves,

onions, green chilies, turmeric, and a pinch of salt. Some versions include peanuts

for a crunchy texture or potatoes for more heartiness. A dash of lemon juice and fresh

coriander on top adds a burst of freshness

INGREDIENTS

  • Flattened rice
  • Onion,Green chilli,Turmeric
  • Curry leaves
  • Lemon juice
  • Mustard seed
  • Peanuts(optional)
  • Coriander

    • STEPS

    1. Rinse poha and keep aside.
    2. In oil, temper mustard seeds, curry leaves, and peanuts.
    3. dd chopped onions and green chilies, cook until soft.
    4. Add turmeric, salt, then mix in poha.
    5. queeze lemon, garnish with coriander. Done!
